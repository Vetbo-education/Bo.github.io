Bo Chen

Veterinary Anatomic Pathologist

Introduction

Bo Chen is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Pathologists (ACVP), certified as a Veterinary Anatomic Pathologist from September 30, 2023, to September 30, 2033. With over three years of experience as a veterinary pathologist and non-clinical pharmacologist, Bo has worked at WuXi AppTec Co., Ltd. in Suzhou, China, a leading global pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical R&D contract research organization (CRO). Bo specializes in organizing and overseeing toxicologic and investigative pathology studies for preclinical drug development, ensuring compliance with Good Laboratory Practices (GLPs) and regulatory guidelines from the FDA, OECD, and NMPA. Bo has conducted detailed gross and microscopic examinations, authored over 120 comprehensive pathology reports, and collaborated with multidisciplinary teams to evaluate safety profiles for drug approvals.

Currently, Bo is a PhD candidate in Veterinary Pathology at the University of Melbourne, focusing on canine atopic dermatitis using advanced canine skin organoid systems. Bo holds a Master's degree in Molecular and Comparative Pathobiology from National Taiwan University and a Bachelor's degree in Veterinary Medicine from Zhejiang University. Bo has published several SCI-indexed papers, including studies on organoid technology, feline mammary carcinomas, and canine renal nephroblastoma. Recognized with numerous honors, such as the 2024 Science Abroad Travelling Scholarships and the 2020 China Scholarship Council - University of Melbourne PhD Scholarship, Bo is also an Active Student Member of the Society of Toxicologic Pathology (2024-2025).
